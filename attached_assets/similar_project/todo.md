## Design Details
- Tool should research destinations on demand
- Focus on places within 3 hours of Somerset, NJ
- Include information from social media and official websites
- Provide comprehensive details about:
  - Overview of the attraction/destination
  - Main attractions and activities
  - Estimated costs
  - Best times to visit (year and day)
  - EV parking and accessibility
  - Insider preparation and tips
  - Travel time estimates from Somerset, NJ
  - Top 3 highest-rated places to eat around the area
